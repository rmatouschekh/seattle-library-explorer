I'm having problems with:

Home

The search input is applied to both the Title and Author fields and is case-insensitive. Thus "Rowling" might get results for J.K. Rowling or books with "Prowling" in the title.

For more controlled searching, go to Advanced Search.

Advanced Search

The Title and Author fields will only restrict results to those than contain your search string. For example, searching with the title field as "hat" could return books with titles ranging from "Century of Hats" to "Whatever".

If a publication year is given, only exact matches with that field will be displayed.

Note that at most 25 results are displayed.

What's Most Popular?

The "Collections" option in the dropdown menu is different that the Media Format Types. If "Collections" is selected, a table will be displayed with the most popular collections and number of items checked out from them.
